# Shavella Vibrant Design Guidelines

A bold, playful street food design system inspired by modern shawarma and wrap brands. Features hand-drawn illustrations, vibrant colors, and bold typography that captures the energy of urban food culture.

---

## Design Philosophy

- **Bold & Energetic**: High contrast colors, large typography, eye-catching layouts
- **Playful Illustrations**: Hand-drawn style food elements (vegetables, cheese, sauces)
- **Street Food Aesthetic**: Urban, casual, fun, appetizing
- **Multi-Surface**: Works on dark and light backgrounds for versatility

## Typography

### Font Stack
- **Primary**: 'Bebas Neue', 'Impact', sans-serif (headlines)
- **Secondary**: 'Inter', -apple-system, sans-serif (body text)

### Scale
| Level | Size | Weight | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|-------|
| Display | 72px+ | 700 | Hero headlines |
| H1 | 48px | 700 | Section headers |
| H2 | 36px | 700 | Card titles |
| H3 | 24px | 600 | Subsections |
| Body Large | 18px | 400 | Featured text |
| Body | 16px | 400 | Regular content |
| Caption | 14px | 500 | Labels, tags |
| Small | 12px | 400 | Fine print |

### Typography Rules
- Headlines always UPPERCASE
- Tight letter-spacing on display text (-0.02em)
- Line height 1.1 for headlines, 1.5 for body

## Components

### Price Badges
- Rotated pill shape (-15deg rotation)
- Bright background (orange, red, or pink)
- Bold white text
- Slight shadow for depth

### Cards
- Rounded corners (16px)
- Optional illustration background
- Strong color blocking
- Price badge overlay

### Buttons
- Primary: Solid color fill (red, orange, or pink)
- Large padding (14px 28px)
- Uppercase text
- Slight hover scale (1.02)
- Border-radius: 8px

### Illustrations
- Hand-drawn style food elements
- Floating/scattered placement
- Slight rotation for dynamism
- Include: tomatoes, carrots, cheese drops, onions, peppers

---

## Layout Patterns

### Menu Item Card
1. Large product name (uppercase)
2. Rotated price badge
3. Ingredients list (smaller text)
4. Optional illustration elements

### Promotional Banner
1. Bold headline split across lines
2. Central illustration
3. Location/date info at bottom
4. Dark background for impact

---

## Animation & Interaction

- Hover: Subtle scale (transform: scale(1.02))
- Transitions: 0.2s ease
- Price badges: slight bounce on hover
- No heavy animations - keep it snappy

---

## Usage Examples

### Dark Theme (Promotional)
- Black background
- White/orange text
- Illustrated food elements
- Perfect for announcements, events

### Light Theme (Menu)
- Cream or blue background
- Dark text with colored accents
- Clean layout for readability
- Ideal for menus, pricing
