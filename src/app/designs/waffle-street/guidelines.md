# Waffle Street Design System

A bold, playful street food branding system with warm colors, chunky typography, and illustrated elements. Perfect for casual dining, food trucks, and urban eateries.

## Philosophy

Fun meets flavor. Bold typography grabs attention while warm colors create appetite appeal. The design is unapologetically loud yet approachable, perfect for street food that stands out in a crowd.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Cream** `#F5E6C8` - Warm background, paper texture feel
- **Deep Orange** `#E27B30` - Primary accent, headlines
- **Black** `#1A1A1A` - High contrast backgrounds, text
- **White** `#FFFFFF` - Text on dark backgrounds

### Accent Colors
- **Sky Blue** `#9ED6EE` - Secondary backgrounds, highlights
- **Crimson Red** `#E53935` - CTAs, urgent elements
- **Hot Pink** `#E66B9F` - Packaging, fun accents
- **Golden Yellow** `#F5B041` - Price tags, badges

### Supporting Colors
- **Warm Brown** `#8B4513` - Outlines, illustrations
- **Sage Green** `#6B8E23` - Fresh ingredients accent
- **Tomato Red** `#CD5C5C` - Food illustrations

## Typography

### Font Family
```
'Oswald', 'Impact', 'Arial Black', sans-serif (Headlines)
'Inter', -apple-system, sans-serif (Body)
```

### Scale
- **Hero Title**: 72px / Bold (700) / -0.02em tracking / uppercase
- **Section Title**: 48px / Bold (700) / uppercase
- **Card Title**: 24px / Semi-bold (600)
- **Price Tag**: 32px / Bold (700)
- **Body**: 16px / Regular (400) / 1.6 line-height
- **Caption**: 12px / Medium (500) / uppercase / 0.1em tracking

## Components

### Buttons

**Primary (CTA)**
- Background: Crimson Red (#E53935)
- Text: White
- Border-radius: 24px (pill shape)
- Padding: 14px 32px
- Text-transform: uppercase
- Font-weight: 700
- Hover: darken 10%

**Secondary**
- Background: Transparent
- Border: 2px solid Black
- Text: Black
- Border-radius: 24px
- Hover: Background black, text white

### Cards
- Background: White or Cream
- Border: 2px solid #1A1A1A
- Border-radius: 16px
- Box-shadow: 4px 4px 0 #1A1A1A (offset shadow)
- Hover: Transform translateY(-4px)

### Price Tags
- Background: Golden Yellow (#F5B041)
- Text: Black
- Border-radius: 8px
- Rotation: -5deg to 5deg
- Font-weight: Bold
- Border: 2px solid Black

### Badges
- Border-radius: 20px (pill)
- Padding: 6px 16px
- Font-size: 12px
- Text-transform: uppercase
- Font-weight: 600

## Layout Principles

1. **Bold Asymmetry** - Off-center elements, rotated price tags
2. **High Contrast** - Black on cream, white on black
3. **Generous Spacing** - Let bold elements breathe
4. **Illustrated Accents** - Hand-drawn food elements scattered throughout

## Animation

- Duration: 0.2s for interactions
- Easing: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- Hover effects: Lift up with shadow, scale 1.02
- Active: Scale 0.98 (press effect)

## Patterns & Decorations

- Scattered confetti/sprinkle elements
- Hand-drawn squiggly lines
- Food ingredient illustrations (tomatoes, carrots, cheese drips)
- Star bursts for promotions
